Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 1/4 ounce packet taco seasonning
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (26 ounces) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (8 ounce) box spaghetti noodles
  • 12 hard taco shells
  • salsa
  • cheddar cheese
  • lettuce

Recipe Steps

  1. Prepare noodles according to package directions.
  2. Brown meat in pan until no longer pink.
  3. Add taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-6 minutes, until sauce thickens.
  5. Add spaghetti sauce and cook until warmed.
  6. Add to noodles and mix thoroughly.
  7. place a spoonful of salsa in the shell.
  8. Sprinkle with a small amount of cheese.
  9. Finally, finish filling the shell with spaghetti.
