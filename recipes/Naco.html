Naco

A combination of two of the most populer Mexican dishes nachos and tacos.

From the hit cartoon Kim Possible the "Naco" is ron stppables signature choice from Bueno Nacho.

Picture the "Naco" as cheesy nachos rolled up into a soft shell taco.

Ingredients

  • large flour tortilla
  • taco meat
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • cheese dip
  • tortilla chips

Recipe Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cut large flour tortilla into a square.
  3. Fold the two corners of the square tortilla and clip them.
  4. Place the tortilla into a bowl.
  5. Bake the tortilla for 5 to 6 minutes until it is stiff to the touch.
  6. Fill the Naco shell with taco meat, tortilla chips, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese dip.
